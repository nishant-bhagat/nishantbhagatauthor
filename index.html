The Book

The Darjeeling Murders

A mystery novel by Nishant Bhagat, available now on Kindle. Four friends travel to the hill town of Darjeeling to reinvestigate a thirty-year-old killing spree, only to fixate on the one murder the convicted killer still refuses to confess. What starts as a documentary project soon puts them at odds with a town that would rather its past stayed hidden.

About

About the author

Nishant Bhagat writes atmospheric, character-driven mystery and crime fiction about ordinary people who go looking for the truth and find more than they bargained for. The Darjeeling Murders is his debut novel.

An electronics engineer and crime fiction novelist, Nishant spent close to sixteen years in the corporate world before writing his debut novel. He is currently at work on his second mystery novel.
